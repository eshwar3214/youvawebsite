Events

Events Organized by YOUVA

WALK EAT DONATE

● An annual fundraiser conducted by yOUva. ● Raised money via stalls organised by the volunteer. ● All the hygiene measures were taken .

TAARE ZAMEEN PAR

A drawing competition conducted for government school students, to bring joy in their lives via fun games, gifts and 100% participation of volunteers in it.

TREE PLANTATION

The team of YOUVA also tries to make its mandatory contribution to the nature , in the light of which “Tree Plantation” was held. A total of 150 saplings were planted on the grounds of University College of Technology ,Osmania University. Not just students but many faculty members were a part of this event.

ORPHANAGE VISITS

Orphanage visits are a must in every span of 6 months or less in YOUVA. Conducting competitions ,organizing lunch treats ,playing and celebrating festivals with the children and becoming a part of their happy days is something the YOUVA team can never let go off. The orphanages visited: ● Agape Orphanage ,Boduppal ● Kristos Ashram, Dammaiguda

CHIGURU

Chiguru ,organized by Youth for Seva ,is an event which celebrates the talents of disadvantaged and needful students. YOUVA collaborated with Youth for Seva for this event and organized talent hunts and many other fun activities, food and other necessities were also provided during the event.
